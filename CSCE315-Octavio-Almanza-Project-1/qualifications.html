Technical Interests

Software Engineering - Although the branch of software engineering is fairly broad, I have always been interested in the process in which larger-scale solutions are designed, developed, tested, and deployed throughout the industry. This branch is known for demanding collaboration within a team, which enables each member to focus on a particular aspect of the final solution.

Mobile Application Development - Given the rise of smartphones since the early 2010s, mobile applications are more important than ever before. There are so many useful tools and creative uses of the functionality offered by a modern smartphone, from online-ordering applications to Artificial Intelligence assistants. Today, many companies are relying on enterprise applications to communicate both internally and with clients, and I plan to pursue a career involving these solutions.

Cybersecurity - As people entrust more and more of their sensitive information to corporations, applications, and banks, developments in the field of cybersecurity have become extremely important. I have seen family members become victims of identity theft and fraud as a result of data leaks. Because of this, I have decided to pursue cybersecurity for my minor and plan to learn more about network safety and vulnerability management.

Game Design - One of the primary reasons why I became interested in computer science was my curiosity regarding how my favorite childhood games were created. Game design is an art form that combines individual aspects of visualization, simulation, animation, and game theory, that are then creatively expressed with the help of software tools.
